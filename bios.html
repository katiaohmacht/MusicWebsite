Neerav Kumar

Co-Founder and Co-President of Music Measures

Hi! My name is Neerav Kumar, and I am a co-founder of and piano instructor for Music Measures. I am a senior at Princeton University, and I've played piano since I was four years old. I am currently pursuing a certificate in Music Performance for clarinet, continuing my education from the Juilliard School of Music Pre-College Division. My favorite aspect about music is the communal nature of music-making. Although we are currently in a pandemic, I am so excited to help spread the joy of music virtually to keep my community connected.
